Since Plone 4, a new way of theming is standard which allows themers to work much more direct with HTML and CSS.

Unfortunately, you cannot use this in Plone 3.3.x. But an alternative might be to use a standalone service that does a similar job. That also makes it possible to include several back-end applications under one 'look'
